Shinigami spell is a game that is slightly modified version of rocket game almost all of us have played. The objective of the game is killing the shinigami using a spell before they catch you. You get +1 score for every Shinigami killed.

Curious about Shinigami? Shinigami (死神, literally "death god" or "death spirit") are gods or supernatural spirits that invite humans toward death in certain aspects of Japanese religion and culture. Shinigami have been described as monsters, helpers, and creatures of darkness. Shinigami are used for tales and religions in Japanese culture.
