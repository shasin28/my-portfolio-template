About Angika : Angika (अंगिका) is a language spoken primarily in the Anga region of Bihar and Jharkhand states of India. In addition to India, it is also spoken in some parts of the Terai region of Nepal.[1] It belongs to the Eastern Indo-Aryan language family. It is closely related to languages such as Bengali, Assamese, Odia, Maithili and Magahi.

This android application is an effort to teach more people Angika and popularise it.
